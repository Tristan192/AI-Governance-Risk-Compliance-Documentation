# Extra Helpful Documents

This folder contains supplementary templates and tools to support AI governance, risk management, and compliance activities. These documents provide practical resources for audits, gap analysis, inventory management, and compliance tracking.

## Contents

### Audit and Assessment Tools
- **Audit Interview Questionnaire.docx**: A structured questionnaire template for conducting interviews during AI governance audits, covering key areas such as policies, controls, and incident response.
- **Gap Summary.docx**: A template for documenting gaps identified during assessments, including descriptions, impacts, and remediation plans.

### Inventory and Tracking
- **Model Inventory Sheet.xlsx**: An Excel spreadsheet for maintaining a comprehensive inventory of AI models, including metadata, deployment status, and risk assessments.
- **Unified Compliance Requirement Summary.xlsx**: A centralized tracker for mapping compliance requirements across different regulatory frameworks and standards.

## How to Use

### Conducting Audits
1. **Preparation**: Use the Audit Interview Questionnaire to prepare questions tailored to your organization's AI governance framework.
2. **Interviews**: Conduct structured interviews with key stakeholders, documenting responses and follow-up questions.
3. **Analysis**: Compile findings and identify gaps using the Gap Summary template.

### Managing AI Assets
1. **Inventory Creation**: Populate the Model Inventory Sheet with details of all AI models in use or development.
2. **Regular Updates**: Maintain the inventory through regular reviews and updates as new models are deployed or retired.
3. **Risk Integration**: Link inventory entries to risk assessments and compliance requirements.

### Compliance Tracking
1. **Requirement Mapping**: Use the Unified Compliance Requirement Summary to map requirements from various standards (e.g., GDPR, NIST AI RMF).
2. **Status Monitoring**: Track implementation status, evidence collection, and audit readiness for each requirement.
3. **Gap Analysis**: Identify areas needing additional controls or documentation.
